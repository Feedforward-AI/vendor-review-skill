# Adapt - Vendor Agility
Evaluate the ADAPT (Vendor Agility) criterion for this vendor.

Assess how quickly the vendor incorporates advances in the AI landscape:
- How rapidly do they adopt new foundation models when superior options emerge?
- Do they update their approaches as new techniques and paradigms develop?
- Is there evidence of continuous improvement aligned with the pace of AI advancement?
- What is their typical update cycle compared to frontier model releases?

Score as:
- "yes": Rapid adoption of new capabilities, keeps pace with AI advancement
- "partially": Updates occur but lag behind frontier capabilities
- "no": Slow to adopt new models and techniques
- "insufficient": Cannot assess due to lack of information

Provide:
1\. A 2-4 sentence assessment with specific evidence
2\. Trade-off statement (what org gains vs gives up)
3\. 2-3 specific questions to ask the vendor if gaps exist
