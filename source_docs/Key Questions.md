# Key Questions
Based on the evaluation, generate 5-6 strategic questions that decision-makers should consider based on their organizational context and priorities.

These questions should help the reader determine if this tool is right for THEIR organization specifically.

Example questions:
- How critical is [pain point] as an immediate priority versus a problem you could solve incrementally?
- Is your organization actively building AI capacity now, or is that a future priority?
- What is your tolerance for vendor lock-in given the rapid pace of AI advancement?
- Would this tool be a primary AI touchpoint for employees, or part of a portfolio approach?
- How do you evaluate the trade-off between immediate productivity gains and long-term capability development?
