# Use - Genuine Utility

Evaluate the USE (Genuine Utility) criterion for this vendor.

Assess whether the tool delivers genuine value versus the appearance of value:
- Does it produce measurably better outcomes than alternatives (including simpler approaches)?
- Is there evidence of real productivity gains, or does it merely feel productive?
- Does it solve problems that actually matter to the business?
- Are there customer case studies or testimonials demonstrating concrete value?

Score as:
- Pass: Clear evidence of genuine utility and measurable value
- Partial: Some utility demonstrated but with caveats or limitations
- Fail: Appearance of value without substance
- Insufficient: Cannot assess due to lack of information

Provide:
1\. A 2-4 sentence assessment with specific evidence
2\. Trade-off statement (what org gains vs gives up)
3\. 2-3 specific questions to ask the vendor if gaps exist

## Output contract
Assess ONLY this dimension, ONLY through the one question (build or erode independent AI capacity). This is NOT a generic software review — do not comment on security, pricing, market share, or support SLAs except where they bear on this dimension's capacity question. Marketing claims are not evidence. Cite every claim to a dossier fact id. Return exactly the `dimension-result` schema.
